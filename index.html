hi hi! i'm milah

I'm Milah Estillore, an illustrator & graphic designer based in Canada. For the past six years, I've been creating cozy characters, bold graphics, and story-driven visuals for festivals, events, and online clients.

I work across digital and traditional media, from posters and branding to manga layouts, Live2D rigs, and game assets. Currently studying at Syn Studio's prestigious concept art program in Montreal!

What I love to work on:

  • Character-driven illustrations & key art
  • Manga & comic page layouts
  • Posters, event branding, and social graphics
  • UI/UX-adjacent layout and visual systems
  • Game art and 2D/3D concept sketches

I'm currently open for work as a mangaka's assistant or as a graphic designer.
